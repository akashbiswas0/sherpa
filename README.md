# Delhi Tourism

Delhi, the capital city of India, is a bustling metropolis that showcases a unique blend of history and modernity. Known for its rich heritage, you can explore historical sites like the Red Fort, India Gate, and Qutub Minar. The city offers a diverse culinary scene, vibrant markets, and is a great starting point for exploring other parts of India. From street food tours to cultural festivals, Delhi has something for every traveler.
