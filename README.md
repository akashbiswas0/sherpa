## Mt. Everest
1. Mt. Everest is the highest mountain in the world, with a peak that reaches 8,848 meters (29,029 feet) above sea level.
2. It is located in the Himalayas on the border of Nepal and the Tibet Autonomous Region of China.
3. The first successful ascent was achieved by Sir Edmund Hillary and Tenzing Norgay on May 29, 1953.
4. The mountain attracts many climbers each year, from highly skilled mountaineers to inexperienced tourists.
5. Climbing Mt. Everest poses extreme risks, including altitude sickness, weather conditions, and avalanches.
